MyErickson

My Erickson is an app designed for residents to enhance their daily living experiences within the community. With this app, you can:

  • View dining menus and make dinner reservations.
  • Learn about the General Services department and submit work orders electronically.
  • Access staff and resident directory information.
  • Check daily activities and, in some communities, register to participate in events.

Please note that Grandview Exchange and MyErickson are not connected in any way. Grandview Exchange is run by residents, reservists, and Priority List members. MyErickson is owned and managed by Erickson.
