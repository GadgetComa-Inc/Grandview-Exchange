Finding the Site & Logging In

Sometimes you already have Grandview Exchange set up, but you’re not sure how to get back to it. This page shows you how to start Grandview Exchange depending on how you usually get to it — the app, a web browser, or your email.

Find Your Method

Select your preferred access method below to see targeted step-by-step instructions for getting started right away.

If you use the Groups.io app

Open the Groups.io app on your phone or tablet. If the app asks you to log in, follow the prompts. Once you’re logged in, tap Your Groups and choose Grandview Exchange.
Step-by-Step Instructions for the App →

If you use a web browser

Open Grandview Exchange from your bookmark, desktop shortcut, or home‑screen icon. If the site asks you to log in, follow the prompts. After logging in, you’ll land on the Home Group Start Page.
Step-by-Step Instructions for the Web Browser →

If you use your email

You don’t need to log in or open the website. To post a message, send an email to:
home@grandviewexchange.groups.io

To reply, simply reply to any Grandview Exchange email.
