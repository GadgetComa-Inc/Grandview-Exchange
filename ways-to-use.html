Ways to Use Grandview Exchange

Here are some of the most common things residents do on Grandview Exchange, along with quick examples to help you get started.

Ask a Question

Residents often post questions about services, recommendations, or community information.

Examples:

  • “Does anyone know the schedule for the shuttle this week?”
  • “Who do I contact for a maintenance request?”
  • “Any recommendations for a nearby dentist?”
How to Post

Share Something Useful

Residents share tips, reminders, and helpful information with neighbors.

Examples:

  • “The package room is very busy today — expect a short wait.”
  • “The pool heater is being repaired this afternoon.”
  • “The local pharmacy is offering flu shots this week.”
Learn How

Buy, Sell, or Give Away Items

Residents frequently post items they no longer need or items they’re looking for.

Examples:

  • “Free: set of wine glasses — pickup in the lobby.”
  • “Looking for a small bookshelf for my den.”
  • “Selling a gently used desk chair.”
Post an Item

Join a Club or Activity

Many clubs and interest groups use Grandview Exchange to coordinate meetings and share updates.

Examples:

  • Book Club announcements
  • Walking Group schedules
  • Mahjong or Bridge meetups
