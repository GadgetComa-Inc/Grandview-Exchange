Choose How You Want to Use Grandview Exchange

You can use Grandview Exchange in three different ways — the Groups.io app on your phone or tablet, your favorite web browser on any device, or your email. Each one works well — the best choice depends on what device you like to use and how you prefer to read and send messages.

Choose the method that feels most comfortable for you.

Ways to Use Grandview Exchange

Use the Groups.io App
The Groups.io app lets you read and post messages on your phone or tablet. Once installed, it stays signed in and is easy to open anytime.

This is a good choice for you:

  • If you use a phone or tablet frequently
  • If you like opening an app from your home screen
  • If you want something that stays signed in and ready

Drawback: If you prefer a larger screen, the app may feel a bit small.

Get Started With the App
Use Your Favorite Browser
Using a browser gives you the full Grandview Exchange website on a larger screen. This is the easiest way to read messages and explore the site.

This is a good choice for you:

  • If you like using a computer or tablet with a larger screen
  • If you prefer seeing more information at once
  • If you’re comfortable clicking links or using bookmarks

Drawback: You may need to type the website address or use a bookmark.

Get Started With Your Browser
Use Your Email
You can receive Grandview Exchange messages directly in your email inbox and reply just like any other email.

This is a good choice for you:

  • If you already read email every day
  • If you prefer everything to come directly to your inbox
  • If you want to reply just like any other email

Drawback: It’s harder to browse older messages or explore groups.
