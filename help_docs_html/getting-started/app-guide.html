Using the Groups.io App

The Groups.io app is a simple way to read and post messages on your phone or tablet. Once installed, it stays signed in and is easy to open anytime.

This is a good choice for you:

  • If you already read email every day
  • If you prefer everything to come directly to your inbox
  • If you want to reply just like any other email

Drawback: It’s harder to browse older messages or explore groups.

What you can do with the app

  • Read messages from your neighbors
  • Post new messages
  • Reply to conversations
  • View photos and attachments
  • Browse groups and topics
  • Receive notifications when new messages arrive

How to get started

  1. Open the App Store (iPhone/iPad) or Google Play (Android).
  2. Search for “Groups.io”.
  3. Install the app.
  4. Open the app and sign in.
    If you don’t remember your password, you can request a sign‑in link.
  5. Tap “Grandview Exchange” to open the group.
  6. Optional: Add the app to your home screen dock so it’s easy to find.

Tips for easier use

  • You can increase your phone or tablet’s text size if the messages feel small.
  • The app keeps you signed in, so you won’t need to enter your password each time.
  • You can turn notifications on or off depending on how many alerts you want.

Troubleshooting

  • I can’t find the app: Make sure you search for “Groups.io” (with a dot).
  • I forgot my password: Tap “Email me a link to sign in”.
  • I don’t see Grandview Exchange: Make sure you’re signed in with the same email you used to join.
