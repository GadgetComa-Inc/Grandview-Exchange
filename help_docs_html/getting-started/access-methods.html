Access Grandview Exchange

Residents can use the community in a browser, through email digests, or by saving a shortcut for quick access.

Use the website

Open the Grandview Exchange website in your browser to read updates, join discussions, and manage community activity. The site works well on desktop and laptop computers.

Use email digests

Email delivery lets residents stay in touch even when they are not actively browsing. Adjust email preferences in account settings to receive the level of updates that works best.

Create a shortcut

Save the site to your desktop or favorites for faster access. This is especially helpful for residents who use the community regularly.
