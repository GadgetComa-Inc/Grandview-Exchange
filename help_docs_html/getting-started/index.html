Getting Started with Grandview Exchange

Welcome! This page will help you understand what Grandview Exchange is, how you can access it, and how to choose the method that works best for you.

What is Grandview Exchange?

Grandview Exchange is the online gathering place for residents of The Grandview. It’s where neighbors share announcements, ask questions, offer recommendations, post items for sale, and stay connected with what’s happening in the community.

You can use it on a computer, tablet, smartphone, or even entirely through email — whichever is most comfortable for you.

How Can I Access It?

You can use Grandview Exchange in three different ways. Each one works well — the best choice depends on what device you like to use.

Use the App
Best for iPhone, iPad, and Android users who prefer a simple, app‑based experience. Install the app, sign in once, and you’re ready to go.
Open App Guide
Use the Browser
Best for laptops, desktops, and iPads. This is the easiest way to read messages, browse groups, and navigate the site.
Open Browser Guide
Use Email
Best for residents who prefer to read and reply directly from their inbox. No website or app required.
Open Email Guide

How Do I Choose the Best Way?

Here’s a simple guide to help you decide:

  • If you like using your phone or tablet, choose the App.
  • If you prefer a bigger screen, choose the Browser.
  • If you’re most comfortable with email, choose Email.
  • If you want the easiest way to get started, choose the Browser.
  • If you want the simplest way with no passwords, choose Email.

There’s no wrong choice — and you can switch anytime. Many residents use more than one method depending on where they are and what device they have with them.
