What is Grandview Exchange?

Grandview Exchange is the community hub where residents connect, share updates, join club conversations, and stay informed.

Purpose

It is the neighborhood-style online space for announcements, conversations, and community building. Residents use it to stay connected with one another, with clubs, and with local events.

Community model

  • Home Group: the main community space for broad updates and casual conversation.
  • Subgroups: smaller communities for clubs, interests, and focused discussions.
  • Categories and hashtags: labels that help organize messages and make them easier to find.

Next steps
