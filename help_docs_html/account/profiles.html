Create and update profiles

Your profile helps other residents learn more about you and connect with you in the community.

Create your Grandview Exchange profile

Use your Grandview Exchange profile to share details about yourself, add a photo, and control who can see your information.

  1. Go to the Grandview Exchange Home Group and click your name in the upper right corner.
  2. Click “Settings.”
  3. Click “Identity” on the left side of the page.
  4. Fill in your profile details, including photo, username, display name, privacy, bio, and location.
  5. Set Profile Privacy to “Other members of your groups.”
  6. Click “Update Overall Profile” to save your changes.

Important: Do not set Profile Privacy to “Public,” because that would make your profile visible to anyone outside Grandview Exchange.

Update your MyErickson profile

The MyErickson app offers another way to share information about yourself. Use it to update your photo and profile details.

  1. Open the MyErickson app and click the round person button in the upper right corner.
  2. Click “My Profile.”
  3. Add or update your information and photo.
  4. Click “Save.”
