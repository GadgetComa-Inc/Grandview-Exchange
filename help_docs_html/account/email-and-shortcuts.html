Email settings and quick access

Control how much email you receive from Grandview Exchange, and save the site to your device for easy access.

Change how many emails you receive

  1. Go to the group or subgroup you want to change.
  2. Click “Subscription” and then “Settings.”
  3. In “Email Delivery,” choose your preferred option.
  4. Choose one of these delivery modes: Individual Messages, Full Featured Digest, Daily Summary, or Special Notices Only.
  5. Scroll down and click “Save.”

Add Grandview Exchange to Apple devices

On iPhone and iPad, open Safari, tap Share, and choose Add to Home Screen. On desktop and laptop Safari, drag the URL from the address bar onto the desktop or use the bookmarks menu.

Apple users can also add the site to Favorites using the plus button or Command + D.

Add Grandview Exchange to Android devices

Open Chrome, go to the site, tap the three-dot menu, and choose Add to Home screen. Confirm the shortcut name and place it on the home screen.

Add Grandview Exchange to Windows desktop

Open the browser, drag the padlock or globe icon from the address bar directly onto the desktop to create a shortcut.
