Join or leave a subgroup

Subgroups help residents organize around clubs, interests, and focused conversations.

Join a subgroup

Browse the subgroup list, choose a group that fits your interests, and follow the join prompt. Open interest groups are often simpler to join, while club groups may require approval.

Leave a subgroup

Open the subgroup page and use the leave or unsubscribe option if you no longer want to receive that group’s activity.
