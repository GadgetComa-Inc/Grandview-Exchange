Getting Started with Grandview Exchange

Welcome! This page explains what Grandview Exchange is and the different ways you can use it. Choose the method that feels most comfortable for you.

What is Grandview Exchange?

Grandview Exchange is the online gathering place for residents of The Grandview. It’s where neighbors share announcements, ask questions, offer recommendations, post items for sale, and stay connected with community life.

You can use it on a computer, tablet, smartphone, or entirely through email — whichever feels easiest for you.

Different Ways to Use Grandview Exchange

You can use Grandview Exchange in three different ways. Each one works well — the best choice depends on what device you like to use and how you prefer to read and send messages.

Use the Groups.io App
The Groups.io app lets you read and post messages on your phone or tablet. Once installed, it stays signed in and is easy to open anytime.

What’s best for me?

  • Advantages: Easy to open, simple interface, works well on phones and tablets.
  • Disadvantages: Smaller screen, may require learning a few app buttons.
Get Started With the App
Use Your Favorite Browser
(Firefox, Chrome, Safari, etc.)
Using a browser gives you the full Grandview Exchange website on a larger screen. This is the easiest way to read messages and explore the site.

What’s best for me?

  • Advantages: Large screen, easiest to navigate, best for reading long messages.
  • Disadvantages: Requires typing the website address or using a bookmark.
Get Started With Your Browser
Use Your Email
(Gmail, Hotmail, Apple Mail, etc.)
You can receive Grandview Exchange messages directly in your email inbox and reply just like any other email.

What’s best for me?

  • Advantages: No website or app needed, familiar and simple.
  • Disadvantages: Harder to browse older messages, inbox may get busy.
