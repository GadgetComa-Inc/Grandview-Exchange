Grandview clubs

The Grandview Bethesda has a thriving network of clubs. Resident Services can help residents start new groups, schedule space, and support publicity and setup.

Club overview

Residents are encouraged to join clubs that match their interests. If a club does not yet exist, residents can start one with support from Resident Services.

  • Room scheduling and setup
  • Meeting resources
  • Publicity support
  • TV and audio/visual support

Bocce Club

The Bocce Club brings residents together through fun and exercise, with participation tailored to each resident’s comfort level. It has a dedicated subgroup on Grandview Exchange.

Bridge Club

The Bridge Club brings residents together through bridge, with a welcoming environment for residents of different ages and experience levels.

Computer/IT Club

The Computer/IT Club helps members become more comfortable with computers, laptops, tablets, smartphones, and tech topics including AI.

Fiction Book Clubs

Fiction book clubs are facilitated reading groups that value discussion, respect, and open conversation about books and stories.
