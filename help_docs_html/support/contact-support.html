Contact support

When a problem cannot be solved from the help pages, use the support contact options to ask for guidance.

When to contact support

Reach out when you are locked out, cannot post, or need help with an account or policy issue that is not covered here. Support can also help with unusual platform problems.

What to include

  • A description of the problem
  • The page or action that caused the issue
  • The device or browser being used
  • Any error message shown on screen
