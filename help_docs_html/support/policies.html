Policies and rules for members

Grandview Exchange is intended to foster connection, communication, and collaboration. These policies help ensure a welcoming environment.

Overview

By joining Grandview Exchange, members agree to follow the community’s policies and use the platform respectfully. Members are responsible for what they post and for maintaining a secure, respectful environment.

General policies and rules

  • Use Grandview Exchange for communication, information sharing, and community building.
  • Be respectful and considerate of others.
  • Do not use inflammatory, abusive, or offensive language.
  • Commercial activity is not permitted.

Posting rules

  • Avoid controversial, divisive, or polarizing topics.
  • Focus on helpful information, event planning, requests, recommendations, and friendly connection.
  • Keep posts respectful and on topic.
