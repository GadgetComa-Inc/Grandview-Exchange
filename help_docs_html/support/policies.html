Introduction

The Grandview Exchange is intended to foster connection, communication, and collaboration among residents in our Grandview community. To maintain a welcoming and respectful environment, we’ve developed these Policies and Rules to ensure that every member of Grandview Exchange knows what is expected of them and feels comfortable participating.

By joining Grandview Exchange (i.e., becoming a Member), you hereby

  • confirm that you are a resident, have reserved an apartment (i.e., are a reservist), or are on the Priority List of The Grandview Bethesda, and that you have read and understood these Policies and Rules,
  • agree to abide by these Policies and Rules in practice and in spirit, including any future amendments, which will be made available on Grandview Exchange’s “Wiki” (available using the Wiki link in the menu on the left side of the page),
  • take full responsibility for anything you do or post using the “Grandview Exchange’s facilities” (including the Grandview Exchange Home Group, subgroups, and related resource), and
  • recognize that your continued access to Grandview Exchange’s facilities may be limited or revoked as a result of repeated or severe violations of these Policies and Rules.

Furthermore, you acknowledge that

  • certain personal information about you (e.g., email address, your posted messages that may contain personal information about you, such as your activities and interests) will be used as part of the operation of Grandview Exchange,
  • the Administrator and other Grandview Exchange members helping to operate Grandview Exchange (for your benefit and voluntarily) will endeavor to maintain a secure environment for all information on the Grandview Exchange, but cannot guarantee the privacy of information you make available,
  • agree to resolve, respectfully and helpfully, any dispute or disagreement arising out of the operation of Grandview Exchange and these Policies and Rules, and that any unresolved dispute or disagreement will be finally resolved exclusively through a complaint submitted to the Grandview Exchange Governance Council [to be established].

This Policies and Rules document consists of three sections:

  • General Policies and Rules members must follow,
  • Policies and Rules for posting messages on Grandview Exchange, and
  • Privacy Policies of Grandview Exchange.

General Policies and Rules

Purpose of Grandview Exchange - Grandview Exchange is intended solely for communication, information sharing, and community building between and among Grandview Exchange Members. It is not a forum in which third parties outside of the Grandview Community should be invited or involved. Grandview Exchange is not an official channel for submitting requests or grievances to Grandview management, which does not sponsor and has not been involved in the creation of Grandview Exchange.

Standards of Behavior - In all matters regarding Grandview Exchange, members must be respectful and considerate of other members, and not engage in name-calling, or use disparaging, abusive, or offensive language against any individual, group, or entity, including, but not limited to, other Grandview Exchange members, Grandview residents, and Grandview staff members.

Commercial Activity Prohibited - Use of Grandview Exchange for commercial activity is not permitted. This includes advertising or soliciting services for profit or offering to sell items not owned by the Member.

Policies and Rules for Posting Messages

No Controversial or Divisive Topics - A_void posting or participating in discussions that may be polarizing or provoke conflict or discomfort for other members, including but not limited to, partisan politics, differences in religious views, racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination. Why? Because our goal is to focus on what brings us together, not what might divide us.

Grandview Exchange is not a debating society. It will be more valuable to members if we avoid debate about provocative or divisive topics. There are other forums or listservs available for discussing such matters.

Focus on Positive Engagement - Use Grandview Exchange to share helpful information; plan events; ask for information, recommendations, or advice; or connect with fellow members. If you’re unsure whether the subject or content of your message is appropriate, ask yourself, "Does this contribute positively to the community and the goals of Grandview Exchange? Is this respectful to everyone, regardless of their beliefs or background?"

Zero Tolerance for Offensive Behavior - Posts containing offensive language, personal attacks, or any form of harassment are prohibited.

No Impersonation - Do not impersonate any person or entity, or misrepresent your affiliation with a person or entity.

Avoid Defamatory Statements - Statements about an individual or business (such as a restaurant) that are false or contain knowingly erroneous information should never be made and could expose you to civil liability if they damage the reputation of that individual or business. If you express a negative opinion about an individual or business in a posted message, you should avoid such false or erroneous information and must bear full responsibility for the consequences of your message.

No Spam Or Unlawful Messages - Do not post promotional materials, "junk mail," "spam," chain letters, pyramid schemes, or messages that violate Federal, Maryland, or Montgomery County laws or regulations.

Attachments - Do not attach to a message any document, photo, or other content

  • that violates any intellectual property or proprietary rights of any party,
  • if you do not have the legal or contractual right to share it,
  • if it contains viruses, malware, or any other computer code designed to harm or limit computer functionality, or
  • that creates a privacy or security risk to any person.

No Data Harvesting: Do not collect email addresses, other personal information of Grandview Exchange members or any other content posted on this site.

Keep Messages Local and Relevant: Messages should be relevant to our community and focus on local topics, events, and interests.

Your Privacy

Reliance on [Groups.io's Core Policies](https://groups.io/static/privacy) - The Grandview Exchange is hosted on the Groups.io platform. Therefore, the fundamental aspects of data collection, storage, use, and protection are governed by Groups.io's own policy. The Groups.io platform is highly regarded in the industry for providing quality services to small and large communities in the U.S. and abroad. Unlike Google Groups or Facebook Groups, it does not use your postings or conversations for advertising data or to build marketing profiles and does not track your activities. It is a subscription-based service rather than advertising based. Your email addresses and group content remain private and are not shared.

Groups.io Data Collection - Groups.io collects your login information including your name and email address and uses cookies to operate its service. Most importantly, it collects, uses, and stores your personal information only for the purpose of operating the platform and follows industry standard privacy policies, including those set by the European Union. Its privacy policy states that it implements physical, administrative, and technical measures and safeguards designed to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, alteration, unlawful or unauthorized access, use or disclosure and will provide notification to users and regulators of any breach as required by law. 

Groups.io Cookie Policy - For information about the use of cookies on the Groups.io platform, including the types of cookies used (first-party, third-party, session, and persistent) and their purposes (such as customizing your experience, analyzing usage, and detecting fraud), please consult the Official Groups.io Cookie Policy. This policy also details how you can manage your cookie preferences through browser and device controls.

Groups.io Terms of Service - Your participation in Grandview Exchange is also subject to the Groups.io Terms of Service, which outline general user conduct and responsibilities for all users of the Groups.io platform.

Grandview Exchange Privacy Considerations

Beyond the general Groups.io policies, here are specific privacy considerations for the Grandview Exchange online community:

Information You Share - Any content you upload, post, publish, or display on Grandview Exchange is your sole responsibility. Please be mindful that information you post may be visible to other Grandview Exchange members. While Groups.io explicitly states it will not use any content you provide for any purpose other than to provide, operate, improve, or enhance the Services, exercising caution when sharing sensitive personal information is always advised.

Member Responsibilities - Members using Grandview Exchange must also respect the privacy of other members and non-members. Members should not share the personal information of someone else (including another member) using Grandview Exchange, whether such information was learned through the Exchange’s facilities or otherwise, without that third party’s consent.

Restricted Membership Subgroups - Groups.io allows Grandview Exchange to set up "restricted membership subgroups" to allow the Grandview Clubs to limit their Club messages on to a subgroup available only to Club members. A Club may have additional privacy policies or rules. If you choose to join  a Grandview Club, you will be subject to those additional terms.

Third-Party Links and Services - Neither Groups.io nor Grandview Exchange are responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites or services that you may use in interacting with the Exchange. For example, if you use Google Mail to post or receive messages from Grandview Exchange, Google may have access to that email (as it does for all of your emails) when providing its email services to you.  We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any such third-party websites or services before disclosing personal information on their platforms, as their policies, not Groups.io's nor Grandview Exchange’s, govern their handling of your data.

Enforcement

Grandview Exchange moderators, who are volunteers, will endeavor to monitor messages to ensure these Policies and Rules are honored. These Policies and Rules may also be enforced by Groups.io.

Messages that violate these Policies and Rules may be prohibited before posting or removed after posting. Members who repeatedly post inappropriate messages (something we do not anticipate will happen very often) will be contacted privately and may be temporarily or permanently removed from Grandview Exchange.

If you see a message that violates these Policies and Rules, please report it to the Administrator [ [home+owner@grandviewexchange.groups.io](mailto:home+owner@grandviewexchange.groups.io) ]. Your report will be handled discreetly and with care.
