Mute a topic or thread

Muting helps you stop receiving updates for conversations that are no longer active or relevant.

Mute a thread

Open the thread you want to quiet and use the mute settings to stop notifications for that conversation. This keeps your inbox and alerts more manageable.

Why residents mute topics

  • Busy threads can create too many notifications.
  • Muted topics still remain available to read.
  • It helps residents stay focused on the conversations they care about most.
