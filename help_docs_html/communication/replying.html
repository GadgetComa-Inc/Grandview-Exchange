How to reply to a message

Replying lets you join a conversation, answer a question, or add helpful information.

Steps to reply

  1. Open the message you want to respond to. Click the subject line to view the full message.
  2. Click the "Reply" button. It appears below the message. This opens a reply box.
  3. Write your reply. Type your message in the text box. You can edit or remove quoted text if you prefer.
  4. Choose who the reply should go to.
    • To reply only to the person who posted the message, choose "Sender" in the To box. This is the best choice for quick replies such as Thank you.
    • To reply to the subgroup (so everyone will see your reply), choose "Group" in the To box.
  5. Click "Post Message." Your reply will appear directly under the original message.

Choose who receives the reply

Use "Sender" when you want to reply only to the person who posted the message with a quick personal message such as "Thank you." Use "Group" when you want the entire subgroup to see your response and participate in the discussion.
