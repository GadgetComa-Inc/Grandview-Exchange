How to reply to a message

Replying helps residents join conversations, respond to questions, and add useful information.

Reply steps

  1. Open the message you want to respond to.
  2. Click the “Reply” button beneath the message.
  3. Type your reply in the text box.
  4. Edit or remove quoted text if needed.
  5. Click “Post Message” to publish the reply.

Choose who receives the reply

Use “Sender” when you want to reply only to the person who posted the message. Use “Group” when you want the entire subgroup to see your response.
