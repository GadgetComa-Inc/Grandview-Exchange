How to read messages

Reading messages is easier when you know how the list, threads, and tags are organized.

Message list

The message list shows the latest topics, subjects, and conversation summaries. Use it to scan what is new and click into a topic that interests you.

Threads

Replies stay grouped under the original message so the conversation stays easy to follow. Open a message to read the entire thread.

Hashtags and colors

Hashtags show the category or topic of a message. Color-coded labels help you scan a page quickly and identify what kind of content you are looking at.
