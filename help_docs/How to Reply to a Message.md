Replying lets you join a conversation, answer a question, or add helpful information.

# Steps to Reply

1. **Open the message you want to respond to** 
	 - Click the subject line to view the full message.
2. **Click the “Reply” button**
	 - It appears below the message.
	 - This opens a reply box.
3. **Write your reply**
	 - Type your message in the text box.
	 - You can edit or remove quoted text if you prefer.
4. **Choose who the reply should go to**
	 - To reply only to the person who posted the message, choose "Sender" in the To box. 
		  This is the best choice for quick replies such as Thank you.
	 - To reply to the subgroup (so everyone will see your reply), choose "Group" in the To box.
5. **Click “Post Message” Your reply will appear directly under the original message.**
