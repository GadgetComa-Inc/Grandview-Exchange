Now that you are part of The Grandview Bethesda's online community - Grandview Exchange - make the most of it by telling other members a little (or a lot!) about yourself. The place to do this is on your Grandview Exchange Profile. Once you've created your profile, it will be visible in all the Grandview Exchange groups and subgroups that you've joined.

Creating a profile is easy. Just follow the steps below:

1. Go to the Grandview Exchange Home Group and click your name in the upper right corner.![](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Home/wikiimage/19399)
2. Click on "Settings"
	 ![](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Home/wikiimage/19400)

3. Click on "Identity" on the left side of the page.
	 ![](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Home/wikiimage/19401)

4. Fill in the information on the screen. (You don't have to enter or update information in every section.)
	 - Profile Photo - This photo will appear with your messages and in the Directory.
	 - Username - This is a short name that can be used to mention you in messages.
	 - Display Name - This name is displayed with your messages rather than your full name. 
	 - Profile Privacy - Select who you would like to be able to see your Profile.
	 - Bio - Tell us about yourself - interests, career, life history, etc.
	 - Location - Add your apartment number.
	 - If you have your own website like a photo gallery or blog, you can list it here.

5. Make sure your Profile Privacy is set to "Other members of your groups." This ensures that other Grandview Exchange members can see your profile. 
	 ![](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Home/wikiimage/19436)
	 **_Do not set your Profile Privacy to "Public." This option would make your profile visible to anyone (including people not using Grandview Exchange)._**

6. Click "Update Overall Profile."

That's it! You're done.
