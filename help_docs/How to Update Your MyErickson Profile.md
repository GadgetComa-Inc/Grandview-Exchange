Another great way to share a little about yourself is to update your profile in the MyErickson app. Here's how to do it.

1. Open the MyErickson app and click the round person button in the upper right corner. (If you add a photo to your profile, the generic person button will be replaced with your photo.)
	 ![](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Home/wikiimage/19433)

2. Click "My Profile."
	 ![](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Home/wikiimage/19434)

3. Add or update the information in your profile. To add or change your photo, click the camera button under the spot where the photo goes.
	 ![](blob:https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/21efa617-8f89-4b4a-b999-cd9df91edf99)

4. Click "Save" and you're done!
