To help you navigate Grandview Exchange, think of the community as a digital neighborhood. This guide explains how to find your way around "Main Street" and into the various "Houses" (subgroups) that make up our community.

# ![](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Home/wikiimage/19539)

## 1. Starting at the Hub: Grandview Exchange Home Group

The Grandview Exchange Home Group is the heart of the community, serving as our "Main Street". This is where you will find:

- Community-wide news and announcements.
- General "neighborly chat" and discussions about the community.
- Help and support for using the Grandview Exchange website.

## 2. Finding and Joining Subgroups ("The Houses")

Subgroups are smaller dedicated spaces for specific interests or clubs. To find them:

- **Locate the Subgroups Link:** On the left-hand side of the Grandview Exchange Home page, click the link labeled **"Subgroups"**.
- **Browse the List:** This page displays all the different houses in our digital neighborhood. To make it easier to find your way, the list is **arranged into two distinct sections**:
	 - **Subgroups You Belong To:** This section shows the houses you have already "moved into" and joined.
	 - **Subgroups You Can Join:** This section displays the remaining houses in the neighborhood that are available for you to explore.
- **Choose Your Group:**
	 - **Interest Subgroups:** These are "open houses" for hobbies like technology, food, or entertainment. You can join or leave these freely by clicking the **"Join"** button,.
	 - **Club Subgroups:** These are for official Grandview clubs. These houses have a "doorbell"—you usually must be a member of that specific club, and the club leader will need to add you or approve your request to enter,.

## 3. Navigating Between Your Groups

Once you have joined several groups, it's easy to move between them:

- **The "Your Groups" Page:** This page provides a consolidated list of every group and subgroup you belong to. You can reach it by clicking **"Your Groups"** in the blue header at the top of any page.

## 4. Inside the Houses: Categories (The Rooms)

Inside every "house"—whether it is Main Street (the Home Group) or a specific subgroup—conversations are further organized into **Categories**.

- **Purpose:** A category describes exactly what a message is about. This makes it easier to find and browse messages on a specific subject.
- **The Analogy:** If the subgroup is the "house," then categories are the individual rooms within that house.
- **Examples:** Common "rooms" you might visit include Announcements, Events, Recipes, and Tech Help.
- **Visual Indicators:** When you click the "Messages" link on the left side of any group page, you will see colored boxes next to the subject lines. These boxes show you at a glance which "room" that conversation belongs to.

## 5. Organizing Your Messages: Hashtags (The Labels)

To place a message into the correct "room" so your neighbors can find it, you use a **Hashtag** (also simply called a **"tag"**).

- **What it is:** A hashtag is a word or phrase that starts with the **#** symbol. On Grandview Exchange, "tag" and "hashtag" mean the same thing.
- **How to Assign a Category:** When you are posting a New Topic on the website, you do not have to type the hashtag yourself. You can simply click the **"Add Tag"** button next to the subject line and pick from a list of available categories.
- **Finding Information:** You can click on any colored hashtag box in the message list to instantly see a list of every message tagged with that label. You can also click the **"Hashtags"** link in the left menu to see a gallery or list of all active rooms in that group.

## 6. Returning "Home"

If you ever want to get back to the main **Grandview Exchange Home Group** from any other part of the site:

- **Use the "Your Groups" Menu:** In the blue bar at the very top of any page, click or tap the link labeled **"Your Groups"**.
- **Select the Home Group:** A dropdown menu will appear listing all the groups you belong to. Simply select **"Grandview Exchange Home Group"** from that list to return to "Main Street".
- **Mobile Tip:** If you are using a smartphone or tablet, you may need to turn your device sideways (landscape orientation) to see the "Your Groups" menu in the blue header.
