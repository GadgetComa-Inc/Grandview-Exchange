Grandview Exchange allows you to receive messages posted to the Home Group and any subgroups by email. You can choose to receive all messages, summaries of messages, or only special notices posted by the owner of the group or subgroup. 

The instructions below explain how to change these settings.

1. Go to the group or subgroup whose email settings you want to change. For example:
	 - Grandview Exchange (the home group)
	 - Computer/IT Club
	 - Marketplace
2. Click **Subscription** in the upper left corner of the page. Then click **Settings**.
3. In the **Email Delivery** section at the top of the page, click on the circle next to the option you prefer.
	 1. **Individual Messages** - You receive every message from this group as soon as it is posted.
	 2. **Full Featured Digest** - You receive one email per day containing the complete text of the most recent 12 messages.
	 3. **Daily Summary** - You receive on email per day, listing only the subject of all messages posted in the last day.
	 4. **Special Notices Only** - You receive only important announcements from group owners.
4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the green **Save** button.
