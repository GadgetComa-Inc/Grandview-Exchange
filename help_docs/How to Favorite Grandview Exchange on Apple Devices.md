### Apple Safari Users

#### iPhone and iPad

- Open Safari and go to the website you want to save.
- Tap the Share icon (the square with an arrow pointing up).
- On iPhone, this is at the bottom center.
- On iPad, this is in the top-right corner.
- Scroll down the list of options and tap Add to Favorites.
- Note: This is different from "Add Bookmark," as Favorites appear on your start page.
- You can rename the site here if you want a shorter title.
- Tap Save in the top-right corner.

#### Desktop and Laptop

- The Quick Way: Hover your pointer over the Smart Search field (where the URL is), click and hold the plus (+) button that appears at the left end of the field, and choose Favorites from the menu.
- The Menu Way: Go to Bookmarks > Add Bookmark... in the top menu bar. In the dropdown menu that says "Add this page to:", select Favorites.
- The Shortcut: Press Command (⌘) + D and ensure "Favorites" is selected in the destination folder.

### Google Chrome Users

#### iPhone and iPad

- Open the Chrome app.
- Navigate to the website you want to save.
- Tap the three horizontal dots (...) menu icon.
- On iPhone, this is in the bottom-right corner.
- On iPad, this is in the top-right corner next to the address bar.
- Scroll down the menu and tap Add to Bookmarks (the star icon).
- A small "Bookmarked" notification will appear at the bottom of the screen.

#### Desktop and Laptop

- Open Chrome and go to the website you want to save.
- Click the Star icon at the right end of the Address Bar (URL bar).
- A menu will pop up. You can:
- Name the bookmark: Give it a short, recognizable title.
- Select a Folder: Choose "Bookmarks Bar" to keep it visible at the top of your browser.
- Click Done.
