When creating or replying to a message, you can share a photo or file by attaching it.

# Steps to Attach a Photo or File

1. **While writing a new message or replying to one, use the toolbar just above where you write your message.**
	 - To attach a photo, click the photo button.
	 - To attach a file, click the paper clip button.
		  ![](blob:https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/362096f1-6011-47d5-a7f8-ac8b81d305c8)
2. **Drag the photo or file onto the popup window that appears, or click the link to choose photos or file.**
3. **Click Add to attach the photo or file.**
