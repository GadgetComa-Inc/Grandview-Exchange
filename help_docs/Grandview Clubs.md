# Bocce Club

The Bocce Club is designed to bring residents together, foster connections, and create memorable experiences through fun and exercise. We invite residents of all ages, from 60 to 100, to join us in this excellent venture. Participate as much or as little as you’d like–your involvement is always your choice. But we hope to “See you on the court!”

# Computer/IT Club

The Computer/IT Club, created in July 2023, was one of the first Grandview clubs.  As of February 26. 2026, we have 34 members. The Club is a forum for members to become more knowledgeable about the use of their electronic devices–such as computers, laptops, tablets (like iPads), and smartphones. The Club’s members range from novices on tech issues to people who have been in the tech business or who have been speakers on tech and AI topics at local senior educational centers, such as the Osher Life-Long Learning Institutes at American University and Johns Hopkins.

- Club Leader: Sheldon Hochberg, [poppysan@msn.com](mailto:poppysan@msn.com)
- Computer/IT Club Subgroup on Grandview Exchange

# Fiction Book Clubs

The fiction book clubs are made up of participants, led by a facilitator, who value open discussion, respect for differing opinions, and are open to  reading a variety of books from short story anthologies, to best sellers, to classics, to whatever piques our interest in the world of fiction.  We will collectively select books to read; one for each month, several months in advance.

There are multiple fiction book clubs at The Grandview. Club membership is so popular, the club leaders decided to form multiple clubs with a maximum of 15 members. To join a club, please join the [Fiction Book Club Lobby](https://grandviewexchange.groups.io/g/Book-Fiction-LOBBY).

The Lobby allows residents to express interest in joining the next available club. Resident Services is eager to hear from you as to whether YOU would like to serve as a future Book Club Coordinator. If you are interested, please call them at 301-272-9654.

Once a new club opens for business, the club leader selects participants from The LOBBY. Please click the JOIN link below. The next email will confirm that you are on the list. You will soon receive another email with your assignment to the next club.

# History Club

The History Club is a friendly place for anyone who enjoys exploring the stories, people, and events that shaped our world.
Whether your interests lean toward ancient civilizations, American history, world cultures, famous leaders, local Montgomery County history, or anything in between, you’ll find curious neighbors here who love learning and sharing.
