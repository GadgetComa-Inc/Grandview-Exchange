How to format messages

Formatting helps messages stand out and stay easy to read.

Formatting options

  • Bold for emphasis
  • Italics for emphasis or examples
  • Lists for step-by-step instructions
  • Links for external pages and references
  • Quoted text to keep discussions clear

Formatting tips

Keep messages short, easy to scan, and consistent. Use headings or lists when the message includes several steps or examples.
