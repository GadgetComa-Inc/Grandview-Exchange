Subgroups and categories

Grandview Exchange uses a neighborhood model, with the Home Group as the main gathering place and subgroups as smaller communities. Categories help organize conversations within each subgroup.

Grandview Exchange Home Group

The Home Group is the main community space. It is used for discussions about Grandview Exchange and life at The Grandview Bethesda. Its categories include:

  • Announcements and important messages about Grandview Exchange and The Grandview Bethesda
  • Messages from a Grandview club that would be of interest to all members
  • Events at The Grandview Bethesda
  • Questions, comments, and feedback on Grandview Exchange
  • Questions, comments, recommendations, and general discussion about life at The Grandview Bethesda

Club subgroups

Club subgroups are dedicated spaces for Grandview clubs. Club leaders establish categories and hashtags that fit each club’s focus.

Featured clubs include Bocce, Bridge, Computer/IT, Current Events, Fiction Book Clubs, Mah Jongg, Modern American Canasta, Non-Fiction Book Club, Pickleball, Social Singles, and TV Club.

Interest subgroups

Interest subgroups are open, topic-focused communities such as Entertainment, Food and Dining, and other special areas. They use categories and hashtags to organize discussion by subject.
