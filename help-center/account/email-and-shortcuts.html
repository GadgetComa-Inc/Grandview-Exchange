Change how many emails you receive

Grandview Exchange allows you to receive messages posted to the Home Group and any subgroups by email. You can choose to receive all messages, summaries of messages, or only special notices posted by the owner of the group or subgroup.

  1. Go to the group or subgroup whose email settings you want to change. For example:
    • Grandview Exchange (the home group)
    • Computer/IT Club
    • Marketplace
  2. Click Subscription in the upper left corner of the page. Then click Settings.
  3. In the Email Delivery section at the top of the page, click on the circle next to the option you prefer:
    • Individual Messages - You receive every message from this group as soon as it is posted.
    • Full Featured Digest - You receive one email per day containing the complete text of the most recent 12 messages.
    • Daily Summary - You receive one email per day, listing only the subject of all messages posted in the last day.
    • Special Notices Only - You receive only important announcements from group owners.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the green Save button.

Add Grandview Exchange to Apple devices

iPhone and iPad

  • Open the website in Safari.
  • Tap the Share icon (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom center of the screen.
  • Scroll down the list of options and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Type a name for the shortcut and tap Add in the top-right corner.
  • The icon will now appear on your home screen just like a regular app.

Desktop and Laptop

  • Resize your Safari window so you can see a portion of your desktop.
  • Click and highlight the URL/Address in the search field.
  • Click and drag the URL directly from the address bar onto your desktop wallpaper.
  • An icon with the website's name will appear. Double-clicking this will always open the site in your default browser.

Apple users can also add the site to Favorites using the plus button or Command + D.

Add Grandview Exchange to Android devices

  • Android allows you to do this directly within the Chrome app.
  • Open Chrome and go to the website.
  • Tap the three vertical dots (Menu) in the top-right corner.
  • Select Add to Home screen.
  • Enter a name for the shortcut.
  • Tap Add, then tap Add to home screen again (or drag the icon to where you want it on your grid).

Add Grandview Exchange to Windows desktop

  • Open your browser and go to the website you want to save.
  • Resize your browser window so you can see a portion of your Windows desktop.
  • Look at the Address Bar (where the URL is). To the left of the website address, you will see a small padlock icon or a globe.
  • Click and hold that icon, then drag it directly onto your desktop wallpaper.
  • Release the mouse button. A shortcut will appear with the website's icon and name.
