How to Read This Page

Below are simple explanations of the fields you’ll see in the issue tracker. These help clarify how items are categorized and what each status means.

Field Definitions
📝 Type
Identifies the kind of issue: Question, Feature Request, Technical Issue/Bug, Usability Issue, or Content Issue.
📂 Category
Indicates where the issue occurs, such as Posting & Messages, Email Delivery, Mobile App, Browser/Desktop, Documentation, or Training.
📱 Environment
Shows the platform where the issue appears: Web, iPhone, Android, Email, or Unknown.
⭐ Priority
Indicates urgency: Low, Medium, High, or Urgent. Helps determine what should be addressed first.
🔄 Status
Tracks progress: New, In Review, In Progress, Waiting for Info, Planned, Resolved, or Closed.
🛠️ Resolution Method
Explains how the issue was resolved: Training/Documentation, Site Change, Process Change, No Action Needed, Duplicate, or Other.
